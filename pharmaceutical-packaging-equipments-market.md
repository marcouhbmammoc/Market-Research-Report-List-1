<p><p>The pharmaceutical packaging equipment market comprises three main types: primary packaging equipment, secondary packaging equipment, and labeling & serialization equipment. Primary packaging equipment is used to contain direct contact products, ensuring product integrity and safety. Secondary packaging equipment facilitates the grouping of primary packages for distribution and branding. Labeling & serialization equipment provides necessary information and compliance codes on packages, ensuring traceability and preventing counterfeiting. Together, these categories are essential for maintaining quality, compliance, and efficiency in pharmaceutical manufacturing.</p></p>
