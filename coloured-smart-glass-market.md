<p><strong>The Coloured Smart Glass Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electrochromic Smart Glass</li><li>SPD Smart Glass</li><li>PDLC Smart Glass</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The coloured smart glass market comprises three main types: Electrochromic smart glass, which changes color in response to electrical current, offering dynamic shading and temperature control; SPD (Suspended Particle Device) smart glass, which adjusts transparency with an electric charge, providing instant privacy and glare reduction; and PDLC (Polymer Dispersed Liquid Crystal) smart glass, which switches between opaque and transparent states, enabling flexible light control. These technologies enhance energy efficiency, aesthetics, and user comfort in various applications, including architecture and automotive design.</p></p>
