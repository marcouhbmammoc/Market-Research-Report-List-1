<p><strong>The B type Diagnostic Ultrasound Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fanshaped Scanning</li><li>Linear Scanning</li><li>Mixed Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>B type diagnostic ultrasound devices are categorized into three main market types: Fanshaped scanning, Linear scanning, and Mixed type. Fanshaped scanning utilizes a curved transducer to create a wide view, ideal for abdominal and obstetric imaging. Linear scanning employs a straight transducer, providing high-resolution images, particularly of superficial structures like veins and muscles. The Mixed type combines features of both, allowing flexibility in imaging various body parts. Each type caters to specific diagnostic needs across different medical applications.</p></p>
