<p><p>The Robotic End-of-Arm Tools market includes four main types: electrical, hydraulic, mechanical, and pneumatic. Electrical tools utilize motors for precision and speed, making them ideal for automated tasks. Hydraulic tools provide high strength and are suited for heavy lifting operations. Mechanical tools employ gears and levers for simple tasks with a focus on reliability. Pneumatic tools use compressed air for rapid movements, often in assembly lines where speed is crucial. Each type serves specific applications based on power, speed, and flexibility.</p></p>
