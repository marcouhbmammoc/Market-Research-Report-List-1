<p><p>Learning Management System (LMS) software is categorized into two primary market types: cloud-based and web-based. Cloud-based LMS solutions are hosted on remote servers, offering scalability, flexibility, and easier management, as users can access them from any internet-enabled device without worrying about local server maintenance. Web-based LMS, while similar in accessibility, typically refers to systems that operate within a web browser but may require more specific configurations. Both types enhance e-learning experiences but differ in hosting and management approaches.</p></p>
