<p><strong>The 3D Integrated Circuit Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Through Silicon Via</li><li>Silicon Interposer</li><li>Through Glass Via</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The 3D Integrated Circuit market comprises three primary types: Through Silicon Via (TSV), Silicon Interposer, and Through Glass Via (TGV). TSV technology involves vertical interconnections within silicon wafers, enabling high-density packaging and improved performance. Silicon Interposer integrates multiple chips on a single substrate, enhancing signal integrity and reducing latency. TGV utilizes glass as a substrate, providing a lightweight and cost-effective solution with high insulation properties. Each type caters to diverse applications, driving innovation in advanced semiconductor packaging.</p></p>
