<p><p>The Flame Retardant ABS Resin market is segmented into two main types: Non-halogen and Halogen types. Non-halogen flame retardants use alternative compounds to inhibit combustion without releasing harmful halogenated byproducts, making them safer for the environment and human health. In contrast, Halogen type flame retardants contain bromine or chlorine, which effectively reduce flammability but may raise environmental concerns due to potential toxicity. Both types are utilized in diverse applications like electronics, automotive parts, and construction materials for enhanced safety.</p></p>
