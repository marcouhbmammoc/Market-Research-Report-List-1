<p><p>The Electric Vertical Take-Off and Landing (eVTOL) market encompasses three main types: Tilt-X, Lift+Cruise, and Multi-copters. Tilt-X aircraft utilize tilting rotors for both vertical takeoff and efficient horizontal flight, combining agility and speed. Lift+Cruise designs separate vertical lift and horizontal flight capabilities, often using rotors for takeoff and fixed wings for cruising, optimizing efficiency. Multi-copters rely on multiple propellers for lift, offering simplicity and redundancy, making them ideal for short urban flights and cargo delivery. Each type serves distinct operational needs in urban air mobility.</p></p>
