<p><p>FFP masks are classified into three categories based on their filtration efficiency and protection levels. FFP1 masks provide a minimum filtration of 80% against airborne particles, suitable for environments with low toxicity. FFP2 masks offer higher protection, filtering at least 94% of particles, making them ideal for moderate exposure situations, such as healthcare. FFP3 masks provide the highest level of filtration, exceeding 99%, and are essential in high-risk settings, offering robust protection against hazardous aerosols and fine particles.</p></p>
