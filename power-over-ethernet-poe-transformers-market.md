<p><p>The Power over Ethernet (PoE) transformers market primarily includes Flyback and Forward types. Flyback transformers are commonly used in isolated applications, providing multiple output voltages efficiently and facilitating low-cost design. They are ideal for low to medium power levels. In contrast, Forward transformers are used for higher power applications, offering better performance in energy efficiency but requiring more complex design considerations. Both types support PoE technology, enabling power and data transmission through a single cable while enhancing connectivity solutions.</p></p>
