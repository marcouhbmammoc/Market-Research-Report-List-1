<p><p>The automotive differential market encompasses two main types: all-wheel drive (AWD) and two-wheel drive (2WD) systems. AWD distributes power to all four wheels, enhancing traction and stability, making it ideal for off-road and adverse weather conditions. Conversely, 2WD, which includes front-wheel drive (FWD) and rear-wheel drive (RWD), powers either the front or rear wheels, offering simpler mechanics and improved fuel efficiency for everyday driving. Each type caters to different consumer preferences and driving needs.</p></p>
